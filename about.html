About KAG Ndia Church

Welcome to our church family.

Who We Are

KAG Ndia Church is a Christ-centred church committed to preaching the Gospel, making disciples, and serving our community through the love of Jesus Christ. We welcome people of all ages to worship, grow in faith, and experience God's presence.

Our Vision

To build a Christ-centred community that transforms lives through the Word of God, prayer, worship, and discipleship.

Our Mission

To know Christ, make Him known, equip believers for ministry, and reach our community with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our Core Values

  • Faith in Jesus Christ
  • Prayer and Worship
  • Love and Compassion
  • Biblical Teaching
  • Integrity
  • Community Service
  • Discipleship

Our Pastor

Pastor Ken Wachira

Pastor Ken Wachira leads KAG Ndia Church with a passion for teaching God's Word, prayer, discipleship, and community transformation. His desire is to see lives changed through the power of Jesus Christ.
